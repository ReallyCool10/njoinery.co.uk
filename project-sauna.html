The Regent's Park Retreat

Bespoke Sauna

This sauna was built within a luxury residence overlooking Regent's Park - a private retreat designed to feel like an extension of the home rather than an afterthought. The client wanted a space that combined wellness with craftsmanship, and that's exactly what we delivered.

The interior features precision-cut slatted benching in heat-treated timber, designed for comfort and airflow. Integrated LED strip lighting runs along the bench profiles and ceiling perimeter, creating a warm ambient glow that softens the space. The heater was positioned and plumbed in consultation with the client's wellness consultant to ensure optimal heat distribution.

Every timber element was selected for its thermal properties and finished to withstand the extreme environment of a sauna - high heat, high humidity, years of use. This is a room built to last, and built to feel good every time you step inside.
