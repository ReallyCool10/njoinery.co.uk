The Walnut Residence

Walnut Kitchen & Dining

This kitchen-diner was designed as the heart of a private Bristol residence - a space where material, light, and craft converge. Every element was hand-selected: American Black Walnut for the cabinetry, solid brass for the fittings, and onyx stone for the backsplash panels that catch and diffuse the natural light.

The coffered ceiling, crafted in walnut to match the cabinetry below, draws the eye upward and lends the room a sense of permanence and proportion. Each panel was templated, cut, and fitted on-site to ensure a seamless marriage between the joinery and the architecture of the house.

We believe a kitchen should do more than function - it should feel like the room you're proudest of. Every joint, every grain, every surface in this project was finished by hand to a standard we'd stake our name on.
